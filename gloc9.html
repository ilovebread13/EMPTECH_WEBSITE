GLOC-9

Genre: Hip-Hop

Years Active: 1997 - Present

Bio: Gloc-9, born Aristotle Pollisco on October 18, 1977, in Binangonan, Rizal, is a renowned Filipino rapper and songwriter. Known for his socially conscious lyrics and storytelling, he tackles issues like poverty, inequality, and social justice. Active since the late 1990s, Gloc-9 has received numerous awards and is celebrated as one of the most influential figures in Philippine hip-hop.
